📚 A Bit of Context

The Turing test, proposed by Alan Turing in 1950, was designed to assess a machine's ability to exhibit intelligent behavior indistinguishable from a human. This voice test flips the concept: can an AI distinguish between human and machine voices?

By participating, you're not just testing our AI – you're experiencing firsthand the current capabilities and challenges in voice recognition technology.
